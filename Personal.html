In my personal photography I work to visualise the meditative experience I have whilst taking photographs; for me this is a time of reflection and distraction.
I avoid predetermined social or political agendas and focus on the feeling of images first and foremost.
Working mostly in long term photobook projects, I use photography and my environment to explore fictional narratives usually through pairings and sequences.
